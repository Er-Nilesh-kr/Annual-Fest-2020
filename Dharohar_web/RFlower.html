Rules

Rules and Regulations

• “GARDENING CLUB” OF Quantum University is organizing a Flower Show which is open to all the Universities and Colleges.
• Event is going to held on 20th of march, 2020 at garden area of Quantum University.
• ELIGIBILITY-Competitors who are eligible and interested have to submit forms along with entry fee to Gardening club of Quantum University.
• ENTRIES-Entries will be received on 20th of March from 9 am to 10 am and show will take place till 2 pm.
• JUDGING-Judging will be done on 20th of March by our judge panel.
• Judging will be done on the basis of quality of blooms and health of foliage.
• The decision of the judges shall be final.

GENERAL RULES-

• All the participants have to bring their own pot; you can bring more than one pot.
• All the flower specimens should be fresh, insect-free and well groomed.
• No digging or spoiling of the flower show ground will be permitted. Any exhibitor found doing so shall be disqualified.
• Participants who do not confirm the specifications of their specimen should be disqualified.

DESIGN RULES:


• No artificial flowers, foliage, fruits or vegetables are permitted.
• Due to length of show, no cutting of fruits or vegetables will be permitted.
• Fresh plant material cannot be dyed or treated in any way.
