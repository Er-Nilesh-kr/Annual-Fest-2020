Theme of Project: Innovations for the benefit of Society

About IEEE:


The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) is a professional association with its corporate office in New York City and its operations center in Piscataway, New Jersey. It was formed in 1963 from the amalgamation of the American Institute of Electrical Engineers and the Institute of Radio Engineers. Today, it is the world's largest association of technical professionals with more than 400,000 members in chapters around the world. Its objectives are the educational and technical advancement of electrical and electronic engineering, telecommunications, and computer engineering and allied disciplines.

About Organizer:


IEEE Student Branch was established at Quantum School of Technology, Roorkee in January 2017 for encouraging the students, research scholars and faculties for exploring the information in the field of Mechanical, Electrical and Electronics, and Computer Science for Research and Development purpose. After the approval of the branch in January 2017, we have also taken the membership of one affinity group (Women in Engineering, Approved in March 2017) and one technical chapter (Robotics and Automation Society, Approved in June 2017).

The student branch provides members the opportunity to interact with the researchers and professionals around the world through the various events like workshops, seminars, conferences etc.

With the inception of Quantum University in 2018, the Student Branch has been renamed to IEEE Student Branch at Quantum University and has showcased various events.

About Event:


IEEE student branch at Quantum University, Roorkee invites students and research scholars (Graduates and Post Graduates) from various fields of Engineering, Health Science, Agriculture, Management, Arts to showcase their skills acquired in their respective domain in form of different projects which benefits the society in one or the other way.

Project Exhibition Guidelines and Key dates:


The aim of this event is to showcase the talent of the students through their projects. All the projects will be evaluated by a committee of experts.
1. The students have to participate in the exhibition in a team of maximum of 3 members.
2. The projects will be categorized into static and dynamic projects which may be software or hardware.
3. This event will take place on the 2nd day of the Annual fest (Dharohar-2020) of Quantum University i.e., 20 March 2020
4. The teams are required to apply on the prescribed registration form along with a brief report (not more than 200 words) about their project duly forwarded by their respective department head / coordinator through email latest by March 05, 2020.
5. Each team would be provided a separate space for the display of their project according to the subtheme during the event.
6. Each team can prepare a separate chart consists of flow diagram / process chart, if necessary, in support of their project.
7. The report must contain the following information in the sequence given below:
Title of the Project
Abstract
How the Project benefits the Society
8. All the participants will have to report on March19, 2020 by 2:00 pm in the university campus along with their projects. They must have their institute ID cards along with them.
9. The teams are required to register themselves latest by March 05, 2020 by paying the registration fee as mentioned below.

Judgement Criterion:


The submitted projects would be evaluated on the basis of following points. (Marks indicated against each point)
• Idea of the Project – 10
• Designing of the Project – 10
• Presentation Skills – 10
• How it Benefits the Society – 20
The decision of judges regarding winner and runner up would be final and binding on all. The results would be announced on March 20, 2020.
