Rules

Rules and Regulations:

It is a race of the bots. Your bot needs to be the fastest to complete the given track and leave all others behind. Objectives for track may contain:Rough path, Speed breaker, Ditch, Cliff, Inclination

Rules

1.The bot should be remote controlled (wired or wireless).
2. No IC engine is allowed.
3. Bot dimensions should be strictly under 25cmx25cmx25cm.
4. Battery should be on the bot.
5. The maximum voltage that can be provided is limited to 24V.
6. Pre-fabricated/Lego bots not allowed.
7. There will be a qualifying session with each team getting 2 laps (1 lap in case of excessive entries) out of which the faster lap will be considered.
8. The top 5 teams from the qualifying rounds will make it to the final round.
9. Team members are not permitted to touch either their machines or those of their opponents once the race begins (unless there is need to lift the machine). The penalty for doing so disqualification.
10. The machines are not allowed to leave any loose parts on any part of the arena. Any machine disintegrating during the race will be disqualified.
