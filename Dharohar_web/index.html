The Annual Fest 2020

Dharohar is a student's festival held annually at Quantum University. Dharohar 2020 marks the twelfth edition of this popular event. Over the years Dharohar has provided students a stage to explore and exhibit their creative identities. The event has emerged as a confluence of students for inter-university activities and talent shows. Dharohar is held in four categories.

QUANTUM UNIVERSITY
